## ⚡ Template Tips

### General Advice
- **Choose early**: You can switch templates anytime
- **Test before download**: Always preview PDF before submitting
- **Whitespace matters**: Don't overload any template with text
- **Font sizes**: All templates are optimized for readability at print size
- **Color contrast**: All templates pass WCAG accessibility standards

### For ATS Compatibility
Best templates for beating Applicant Tracking Systems:
1. **Classic** (best) — Simple structure, standard layout
2. **Minimalist** — Clean, no complex styling
3. **Academic** — Structured, traditional format

Avoid for ATS if possible:
- Creative (too much styling)
- Tech (dark theme, special chars)
- Startup (gradients, complex layout)

### For Visual Impact
Best for impressing hiring managers:
1. **Creative** — Beautiful, memorable design
2. **Startup** — Modern, energetic feel
3. **Modern** — Professional two-tone look

### For Conservative Industries
Best for finance, law, government:
1. **Executive** — Formal, corporate
2. **Classic** — Traditional, reliable
3. **Academic** — Scholarly, trustworthy

## 🔄 Switching Templates

In the Builder, templates are displayed as visual thumbnails at the top of the left panel:

1. See 3 main templates (Classic, Modern, Creative) prominently
2. Click any thumbnail to instantly switch
3. Live preview updates on right panel
4. All your data is preserved
5. Download PDF in any template

**Pro Tip**: Try different templates before downloading to see which suits your role best!

---

## ✅ Template Quality Checklist

Before downloading:
- [ ] All sections display correctly
- [ ] Text is readable at print size
- [ ] No content is cut off or overlapped
- [ ] Colors are vibrant and accurate
- [ ] Font styling is applied consistently
- [ ] Spacing/margins look balanced

If anything looks off, switch templates or check your content length.
